dakwak is the easiest and fastest way to translate & localize your website to any language your audience and visitors want.

Simple, Easy & Fast

This is truly plug-n-play. Copy the dakwak code into your website, add the dakwak class to any element in your html, and you are all done.

60+ world languages supported

Yes. You can have your website interface support almost every living language in the world!

User Language Detection

You can tell dakwak to detect the language of your site visitors, and push automatically a translated interface of your site based on their language.

Control the translation

dakwak will translate your interface, but you could override translations by editing phrases and words.

Democratize translation

Give your community the last say by allowing to translate words and phrases from your site.

Assign translators

Assign trusted translators for your site through dakwak under a simple, clean and effective work interface.

Customize Look & Feel

When placing the dakwak drop-down menu for users to switch between languages, you can decide where it appears on your site, and how it looks.

Dakwak widgets

Allow your site visitors to help you translate without leaving your site -- coming soon.

Export your translations

Easily export translations for your site in multiple formats: xml, json, or po -- coming soon.

Privacy Friendly

We provide the options for total privacy and anonymous use of our service, the Duck Doesn't Follow You Around.
